60-Day Calm Guarantee

We want care to feel easier for you and your cat. Try Movaro at home during your normal routine for up to 60 days. If it doesn't make care easier, contact us for a refund or exchange.

  • Reach out within 60 days of delivery to start a return or exchange.
  • For hygiene, we ask that returned items be clean and in reusable condition where possible — but reach out either way; we're reasonable.
  • Refunds are issued to your original payment method once your return is received.
  • Wrong size? Exchanges are simple — see our Size Guide.

How to start a return

Email hello@movaro.com with your order number and a quick note about what's not working. We'll send simple instructions and help you find a better fit if you'd like one.
