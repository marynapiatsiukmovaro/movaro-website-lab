Get The Right Fit

Find your cat's size

A snug, comfortable fit is what makes the calming work. Measure once, choose with confidence — and when in doubt, size up.

How to measure

Two simple measurements

  • 1
    Weight — the quickest way to choose. Use your cat's most recent weight.
  • 2
    Body length — measure along the back, from the base of the neck to the base of the tail.
  • 3
    Girth (optional) — measure around the widest part of the chest, just behind the front legs.

Tip: a soft tape measure works best. If you only have string, lay it along your cat then measure the string against a ruler.
