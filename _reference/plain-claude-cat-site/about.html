Our Story

Calmer care for spicy cats.

Movaro started with a simple belief: routine care shouldn't feel like a fight — for you or your cat.

Anyone who's tried to trim a nervous cat's nails knows the drill: the chase, the hiding, the scratch, the guilt. We lived it too. Vet techs and groomers had calming techniques we didn't — gentle, swaddle-like handling that helped even anxious cats settle. We wondered why nothing like that existed for caring cat parents at home.

So we built it. Working with veterinary input, we designed Movaro around one idea: calm, not trapped. A soft, breathable wrap that gives your cat a sense of security and gives you safe, targeted access for the care they need — nail trims, medication, brushing, ear care and vet visits.

What we believe
