Инструкция · 3 шага (Wrap → Soothe → Groom)
Easier than the towel trick

Calm in 3 simple steps

1Wrap

Lay the cat on the open wrap, fold the sides over, fasten the velcro. Snug, never tight.

2Soothe

Give it ~60 seconds. The gentle pressure does the work — the fight drains out, your cat settles.

3Groom

Open one velcro flap, do the nails / meds / ears, close it — done. Treat & release.
