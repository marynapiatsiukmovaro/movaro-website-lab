Simple, honest answers

Questions, answered

"This would never work on MY cat." +
That's what most owners say — including the ones reviewing the spiciest cats they've owned. The swaddle pressure works with a cat's instinct to feel safe when held snugly, and you're covered by our 100-day guarantee.
Isn't wrapping cruel? Won't it panic more? +
Gentle wrapping is the opposite of scruffing — vets use it because it calms. Soft, quiet fabric, reduced visual stimulation, and a positive-association quick-start so the first time goes smoothly.
What size do I need? +
Kitten, S, M, L and XL — most adult cats are M, large/fluffy cats L/XL. Our size finder matches by weight and build.
Can I use it with meds my vet prescribed? +
Yes — pills, ear drops, subQ fluids and insulin, and it works alongside calming aids like gabapentin. It makes the home care your vet prescribed easier (not a medical device).
What if it doesn't work for us? +
Send it back within 100 days for a full refund — keep the calming spray as our gift. Free US shipping both ways.
