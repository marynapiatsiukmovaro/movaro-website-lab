Questions, answered

What if my cat just ignores it? +
Most cats pounce on the first try — but some need a warm-up, which is exactly why the Wrestle Kit includes a 2nd different toy and a 15-minute warm-up guide. Drag it like prey, rub it so it smells familiar, keep sessions short. And if your cat still won't play within 30 days, we'll make it right — so trying it costs you nothing.
Will it actually protect my hands? +
Yes — your hand sits inside a padded forearm sleeve, so the bunny-kicks, claws and bites land on the toy, not your skin. It's built for play, so you still supervise and keep fingers clear of a determined chomper — we don't claim it's bite-proof, we claim it ends the scratched-up arms, and it does.
Won't this teach my cat to attack me? +
It's the opposite. Wrestling is normal, healthy cat play — the Karate Cat simply gives that instinct a target that isn't your hand or your ankles. You become the fun, not the chew toy.
What size is it — will it fit? +
One universal size (about 53×22cm) that fits most adult forearms snugly and works for cats of every age, kitten to senior. No sizes to pick, no guesswork.
Is it safe? What's it made of? +
A soft polyester plush puppet on a padded sleeve, with no hard parts to worry about. Spot-clean and air-dry. As with any plush toy, supervise rough chewers and retire it if it ever tears.
