Questions, answered

What if my cat won't use it? +

We'll make it right — so trying it costs you nothing.
Which size should I get? +
Four sizes, by your pet's weight: S 20″×20″ (up to 15 lb) · M 24″×24″ (up to 25 lb) · L 30″×30″ (up to 45 lb) · XL 36″×36″ (up to 90 lb). All are 8″ tall. When in doubt, size up — cats love room to sprawl as well as curl.
Is it actually calming, or just cute? +
Both. The high, wrap-around rim covers your cat's back and lets them curl into a tight, secure ball — a naturally self-soothing posture — while the warm, soft fur holds their body heat. Feeling safe and warm is what lets an anxious cat truly relax.
Can I wash it? +
Yes — it's machine washable. Gentle cycle, cold water, then air-dry and give it a fluff to bring the shape back. Easy to keep fresh and hair-free.
Will it slide around on hard floors? +
No — the base is anti-slip, so it stays put on wood, tile or laminate. A bed that doesn't skid helps your cat feel secure.
Will it go flat over time? +
The filled rim is made to keep its shape and spring back after washing, so it stays cozy and supportive — not a flat pancake after a week.
What's it made of? Is it safe? +
A soft faux-fur top with a plush-filled rim and a non-slip base — no hard parts. As with any pet bed, keep an eye on cats that chew fabric. (Final materials confirmed with sourcing.)

Free US shipping, dispatched in 24–48h, typically arriving in 5–8 business days. You'll get tracking by email.
