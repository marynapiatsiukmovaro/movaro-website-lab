Questions, answered

"This would never work on MY cat." +
That's what most owners say — including the ones reviewing the spiciest, most fractious cats they've owned. The swaddle pressure works with a cat's instinct to feel safe when held snugly, not against it. And you're covered by our 100-day guarantee, so trying it on your cat costs you nothing.
Isn't wrapping cruel? Won't it panic more? +
Gentle wrapping is the opposite of scruffing — vets use it precisely because it calms. The fabric is soft and quiet (no scary plastic), it lowers visual stimulation, and most cats settle within a minute. We include a positive-association quick-start so the very first time goes smoothly and builds trust instead of fear.
What size do I need? +
We make Kitten, S, M, L and XL — most adult cats are M, large or fluffy cats L/XL. Our size finder matches by weight and build. (The #1 complaint with other wraps is one-size-fits-all; we fixed that.)
Can I use it with meds my vet prescribed? +
Yes — owners use it for pills, ear drops, subQ fluids and insulin, and it works alongside calming aids like gabapentin your vet may already recommend. It simply makes the home care your vet prescribed easier. It's a grooming & calming aid, not a medical device.

Send it back within 100 days for a full refund — and keep the calming spray as our gift. Free US shipping both ways. That's the Calm Guarantee: the only risk is staying stuck with nail-day battles.
