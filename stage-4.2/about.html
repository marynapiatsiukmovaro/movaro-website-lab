Calm over force.

Movaro started with one belief: a scared cat doesn't need to be fought — it needs to feel safe.

Our story

Nail day shouldn't be a battle.

It started the way it does for most cat parents — the hiss, the claws, the two-person wrestling match, and finally the $150 sedated vet trim and a little guilt. We knew there had to be a calmer way.

So we built one. Working with veterinary input, we designed a gentle swaddle wrap that uses the same calming pressure principle that settles a swaddled baby — so cats feel safe instead of trapped, and everyday care (nails, meds, grooming) finally happens at home, calmly, solo.

Today Movaro has helped calm 10,000+ cats — and we're just getting started.
