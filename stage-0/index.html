Questions, answered

"This would never work on MY cat." +
That's what most owners say — including the ones who left the reviews about the spiciest, most fractious cats they've owned. The swaddle pressure works with a cat's instinct to feel safe when held snugly. And you're covered by our 60-day guarantee, so there's zero risk in trying.
Isn't wrapping cruel? Won't it panic more? +
Gentle wrapping is the opposite of scruffing — vets use it precisely because it calms. The fabric is soft and quiet (no scary plastic), it reduces visual stimulation, and most cats settle within a minute. We include a positive-association quick-start so the very first time goes smoothly.
What size do I need? +
We make Kitten, S, M, L and XL — most adult cats are M, large or fluffy cats L/XL. Our size finder matches by weight and build. The #1 mistake with other wraps is one-size-fits-all; we fixed that.
Can I use it with meds my vet prescribed? +
Yes. Owners use the wrap for pills, ear drops, subQ fluids and insulin, and it works alongside calming aids like gabapentin your vet may already recommend. It makes the home care your vet prescribed easier — it's not a medical device or a substitute for veterinary advice.
What if it doesn't work for us? +
Send it back within 60 days for a full refund — keep the calming spray as our gift. Free US shipping both ways. That's the Calm Guarantee.
