Find your cat's size

A snug, comfortable fit is what makes the calming work. When in doubt — size up.

How to measure
1
WeightThe quickest way to choose — use your cat's most recent weight.
2
Body lengthAlong the back, from the base of the neck to the base of the tail.
3
Chest girth (optional) — around the widest part of the chest, just behind the front legs.
