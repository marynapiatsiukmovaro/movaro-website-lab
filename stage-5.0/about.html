How it all started

It started in chaos.

One scared cat, one nail trim, a living-room wrestling match — hissing, hiding, a guilty owner. Sound familiar?

We looked for a kinder way.

We dug into how vets actually calm cats — gentle, even pressure, never force — and tested wrap after wrap until cats truly settled.

So we made Movaro.

A soft, vet-informed wrap that turns the fight into a few calm minutes — done solo, at home.

What we stand for

A scared cat doesn't need to be fought — it needs to feel safe. So we design gentle, vet-informed tools that let you care for your cat at home, calmly: nails, meds & grooming, without the hiss or the guilt.
