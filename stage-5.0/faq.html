Everything cat parents ask before their first Movaro.

Will it work on MY cat? +
Yes — even on the spiciest cats. Movaro uses gentle, even swaddle pressure (the calm-a-baby principle), never scruffing or force. Most cats settle within about a minute. Not calmer in 30 days? Full refund. (Placeholder copy — refine with PI.)
Isn't wrapping a cat cruel? +
It's the opposite of a trap. The wrap helps a scared cat feel safe, not restrained — the same reason swaddling calms a baby. Quiet, soft fabric, limb-access flaps, and your cat is out the moment you're done.

Sizes run Kitten → XL by weight & build (Kitten <3 lb · S 3–6 · M 6–10, most adults · L 10–14 · XL/fluffy 14 lb+). Unsure? Size up. (Final size chart pending sourcing — PI B4.)
