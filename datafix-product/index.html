Simple, honest answers

Questions, answered

"This would never work on MY cat." +
That's what most owners say — including the ones reviewing the spiciest, most fractious cats they've owned. The swaddle pressure works with a cat's instinct to feel safe when held snugly, not against it. And you're covered by our 30-day guarantee, so trying it on your cat costs you nothing.
Isn't wrapping cruel? Won't it panic more? +
Gentle wrapping is the opposite of scruffing — vets use it precisely because it calms. The fabric is soft and quiet (no scary plastic), it lowers visual stimulation, and most cats settle within a minute. We include a positive-association quick-start so the very first time builds trust instead of fear.
What size do I need? +
We make three sizes — S, M and L — matched by your cat’s weight: S for ~3–8 lb, M for ~8–17 lb (most adults), L for ~17–30 lb. Large or fluffy cats take L. Our size finder picks your fit in seconds. (One-size-fits-all is the #1 complaint with other wraps — we fixed that.)
Can I use it with meds my vet prescribed? +
Yes — owners use it for pills, ear drops, subQ fluids and insulin, and it works alongside calming aids like gabapentin your vet may already recommend. It simply makes home care easier. It's a grooming & calming aid, not a medical device.
What if it doesn't work for us? +
Send it back within 30 days for a full refund — and keep the guide as our gift. Free US shipping. That's the Calm Guarantee: the only risk is staying stuck with nail-day battles.
